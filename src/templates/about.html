About this Website

MOOOOO.ooo is the place where I post ideas or solutions to problems that I was unable to previously find information for online. The focus of these topics is primarily computing and engineering. The website is hosted with the aid of the InterPlanetary File System (IPFS) - a peer-to-peer technology that aims to decentralize the web while simultaneously improving its archivability and speed. About the Owner

My name is Colin Wallace. I am an artist who creates music and drawings, as well as an avid programmer and contributor to open source coding projects. I am currently working towards a BS EE degree at the University of Washington in Seattle, WA. Some notable projects to which I have contributed include:
